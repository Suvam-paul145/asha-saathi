# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **Asha-Saathi**, pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and kind to others
- Giving constructive feedback
- Accepting different viewpoints
- Helping newcomers learn

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission

---

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct
- Taking appropriate action in case of violations
- Ensuring a safe environment for contributors
